## What to expect

We meet at a set spot in downtown St. Louis once a week, usually
in the early evening. We walk in pairs or small groups, carrying
a bag of snacks, water, and seasonal supplies (hand warmers in
winter, sunscreen and water in summer).

When we encounter someone, we ask if they'd like to talk. Many do.
Some don't, and that's fine. We never push. We try to learn names
and remember them for next week.

## What to bring

Just yourself. Comfortable shoes. A willingness to listen more
than you talk. We supply everything else.

## A note on tone

We are not here to fix anyone. We are not here to evangelize, lecture,
or solve. We are here to be present and to learn. If you can show up
with that posture, you'll fit right in.
