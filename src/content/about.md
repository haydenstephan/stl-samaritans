## Who we are

The St. Louis Samaritans started off as a small group of friends —
not a parish ministry, not a nonprofit, just people who decided to
keep showing up.

We were inspired by Christ in the City, a community of missionaries
in Denver who walk the same neighborhoods every week, learn names,
and form lasting friendships with the people they meet. We follow
that model here in St. Louis.

## The Good Samaritan

> "Which of these three, in your opinion, was neighbor to the
> robbers' victim?" He answered, "The one who treated him with
> mercy." Jesus said to him, "Go and do likewise."
>
> — Luke 10:36–37

The Good Samaritan didn't run a program. He saw a person on the side
of the road, stopped, and stayed. We take our name and our approach
from him — notice, stop, come back.

## Our friends on the street

They are neighbors. We listen to their stories, learn their names,
and come back next week. Friendship is the work.
