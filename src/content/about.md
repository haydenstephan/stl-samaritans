## Who we are

The St. Louis Samaritans are an informal group of 8 to 15 friends —
not a parish ministry, not a nonprofit, just people who decided to
keep showing up.

We were inspired by Christ in the City, a community of missionaries
in Denver who walk the same neighborhoods every week, learn names,
and treat the people they meet as friends rather than projects. We
follow that model here in St. Louis.

## Why we walk

The Good Samaritan didn't run a program. He saw a person on the side
of the road, stopped, and stayed. Most of what people on the street
need from us isn't material — it's the dignity of being known. We try
to bring that, and to keep coming back.

## What we believe about the people we meet

They are not problems. They are not lessons. They are neighbors,
worthy of attention, capable of friendship, and they have stories
worth hearing.
